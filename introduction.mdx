## Game Objective

You have **40 seconds** to match all 4 pairs of cards hidden on the casino table. Hover over cards with your VR cursor to flip them. Match pairs to make them disappear. Find all pairs before time runs out to **escape with your soul intact**.

Fail, and the death overlay consumes you.

## Built With

- **A-Frame 1.4.0** - WebVR framework for building 3D/VR experiences
- **HTML5** - Single-page application with embedded JavaScript
- **VR-Ready** - Works with VR headsets and desktop browsers
